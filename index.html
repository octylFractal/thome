    With between 2 to 3 fragments, the number of possible name becomes 124,000,000, but most of them suck. It's best to restrict the number of fragments to two since that seems to be a rather nice number. There's nearly 250,000 combinations, which is still probably more than anyone ever needs. Perhaps a more versatile generator could perform better.

    Most names are pretty bad/okayish in my opinion because the generator isn't very complex. There are some nice ones, like "Thome", the name I chose for this generator.
